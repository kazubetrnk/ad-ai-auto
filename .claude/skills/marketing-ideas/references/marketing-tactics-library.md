## Content Marketing Tactics

### Blog & SEO
| Tactic | Description | Effort |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Pillar content | Comprehensive guides (3000+ words) | High |
| Content clusters | Topic hubs with internal links | Medium |
| Comparison posts | "[Product] vs [Competitor]" | Medium |
| Alternative posts | "Best [Competitor] alternatives" | Medium |
| Data-driven content | Original research, surveys | High |
| Expert roundups | Curated expert opinions | Medium |
| Content refresh | Update old posts with new data | Low |
| FAQ optimization | Answer People Also Ask | Low |
